Attorney at Law
Professor Mark R. Lee is an expert in Antitrust, Corporations, and Securities Regulation. He has taught courses in these fields at 11 law schools in the United States and at law schools in Belgium, England, Germany, and Italy. His publications include Antitrust Law & Local Government and Organizing Corporate & Other Business Enterprises. Professor Lee is an experienced arbitrator for the American Arbitration Association and the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority under whose auspices he has resolved dozens of securities regulation disputes.
